Grossed: $1.128 Billion
The next film on our list is Captain Marvel. 

So far, it’s grossed $1.128 billion dollars worldwide, which is pretty darn good for a film that was only released this year. 

The film was written and directed by Ryan Fleck and Anna Gould, and produced by Marvel Studios. 

Brie Larson, Samuel L. Jackson, Ben Mendelsohn and Jude Law are some of the actors that you’ll find starring in this movie. 

The premise of the film is that Earth is caught in some kind of Galactic conflict between two alien civilizations, and Captain Marvel (Brie Larson) is called in.
So far, Captain Marvel is the twenty-fourth highest-grossing movie of all time!
