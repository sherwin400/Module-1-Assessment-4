Grossed: $1.129 Billion
Spider-Man: Far From Home was released earlier this year and has since gone on to gross an impressive $1.129 billion dollars.  

The film was co-produced by Columbia Pictures and Marvel Studios and is the sequel to Spider-Man: Homecoming, released in 2017. 

It stars many great actors and actresses, including Tom Holland (Peter Parker), Samuel L. Jackson, Jake Gyllenhaal and Marisa Tomei.

It was directed by John Watts and written by Eric Sommers and Chris Mckenna. 

This time, Spider-Man (Peter Parker) is recruited by Mysterio and Nick Fury to face the Elementals during a school trip to Europe. 

To date, Far From Home is the first Spider-Man movie to pass the $1 billion dollar mark.
