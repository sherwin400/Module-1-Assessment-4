Grossed: $1.236 Billion
Also known as Fast & Furious 8, The Fate of the Furious grossed over $1.236 billion dollars since it was released in 2017. 

The American action film, written by Chris Morgan and directed by Felix Gary Grey, is the eighth Fast & Furious film in the franchise. 

You can expect to find most of the normal cast in this one, like Vin Diesel, Dwayne Johnson, Jason Statham, Michelle Rodrigues and Tyrese Gibson. 

In its opening weekend, it grossed over $542 million dollars and went on to become the third highest-grossing film of 2017.
