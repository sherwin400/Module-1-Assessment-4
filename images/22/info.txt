Grossed: $1.148 Billion
The twenty-second highest-grossing film of all time is the 2018 Superhero film, Aquaman. 

Based on the DC character of the same name, Aquaman follows Jason Momoa (Aquaman) as he fights to lead the underwater Kingdom of Atlantis to stop his half brother, King Orm, destroying the surface world by uniting the seven underwater kingdoms.
Since it was first released on November 26, 2018, Aquaman has grossed over $1.148 billion dollars. 

The film was directed from James Wan and was distributed by Warner Bros Pictures and is the sixth film in the DC Extended Universe (DCEU). 

For now, it is the highest-grossing DCEU film ever and was the fifth highest-grossing film of 2018!
