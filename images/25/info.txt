Grossed: $1.123 Billion
Transformers: Dark of the Moon was released in 2011 and has since grossed an impressive $1.123 billion dollars worldwide. 
The film is based on the Transformers toy line and was directed by Micheal Bay. 

The battle continues between the Autobots and Decepticons as this time they’re fighting each other to collect powerful technology from their homeland that crashed on Earth’s Moon. 

Dark of the Moon is the third instalment in the series of Transformer films and is the sequel to 2009’s Revenge of the Fallen. 

This film, however, was not co-produced by DreamWorks like the rest in the series and was only distributed by Paramount Pictures.

Some of the film’s stars include Shia Lebouf, Josh Duhamel, John Turturro and Tyrese Gibson.
